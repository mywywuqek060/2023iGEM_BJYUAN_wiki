Best Plant Synthetic Biology

This award is designed to celebrate exemplary work done in plant synthetic biology. Did you build a project in a plant chassis? Did you submit plant parts to the Registry? This award could also be given to a team working with algae or another photosynthetic chassis. Show us what you made and remember to adhere to iGEM safety guidelines!

To compete for the Best Plant Synthetic Biology prize, please describe your work on this page and also fill out the description on the judging form.

Please see the 2023 Awards Page for more information.
